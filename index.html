What is Sky Fares?

Sky Fares gives the customer ultimate control in searching for the best value flight fares during times that suit you. We are enabling people to search for flights during times that suit them and their schedule.

Mix-and-match airlines

Our search engine will match flights that suit your schedule, regardless of airline. This means you could be flying out with one airline and back with another.

Up-to-date pricing

Modern-day air fares are subject to change price every day. This is why unlike some of our competitors, we will only cache flight prices for 1 day and not for a whole week.

Weekend Flights

Sky Fares has been created to bring users functionality not currently present in other search platforms. With our system, users can specify the days and times that they want to fly. So, for teachers and others who are restricted on when you can take annual leave, you can narrow your search to suit your schedule.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why would I use Sky Fares instead of a more established flight search engine?

    There are a number of reasons. We do not have affiliations with any airline and so can we provide fully independent comparisons. We are giving our users the option to make their flight search as simple or as complicated as they like so they can find the flights they want at the best price available.

  • What airlines do you search?

    We are always working on adding support for new airlines on our platform. For now we have support for Ryanair.

  • How often do prices change?

    It varies from airline to airline. In order to ensure we provide our users with the most recent prices, we only cache our data for a single calendar day.

  • Where can I get the Sky Fares Mobile App?

    We don't currently have one. Until we do, you can add our mobile-friendly site to your phone's home screen by following one of the following guides: iPhone / Android

  • How can I get notified when Sky Fares implements new features or support for additional airlines?

    Feel free to join our mailing list in the page footer. We don't believe in needless spam, so you can be assured any email you get from us is worth reading!
